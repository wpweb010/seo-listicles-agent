SERP Position — The rank Google assigned this page. This is Google's trust signal — if it ranks on page 1, Google trusts it.

Your domain (optional) — Enter your domain (e.g., wpwebinfotech.com). If provided, the tool will automatically scan each listicle/aggregator page and detect if your domain is listed, at what position, and whether it's a backlink or mention. This info populates the Excel columns automatically.

Listicles vs Aggregators: Listicles are blogs/service pages listing companies. Aggregators (Clutch, G2, GoodFirms) are directories — both appear in export, but you'll likely want to approach listicles first.

Authority (OPR) — Open PageRank score 0–100, a free proxy. Blank/low scores are common — OPR doesn't index every domain. Google uses 200+ signals (relevance, quality, UX, freshness) that OPR doesn't measure. Use SERP Position as the stronger signal.
